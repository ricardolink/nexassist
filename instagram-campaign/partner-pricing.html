Partner Program · Go-To-Market Timeline
The Plan
From launch to revenue.
A phased approach — build the supply side first, demonstrate value, then monetize. This protects partner relationships and ensures NexAssist delivers real ROI before charging.
01
Phase 1 · Now — Month 3
Free Partner Onboarding
Onboard the first 10–20 partners completely free. No subscription, no fees. Focus on filling all 9 service categories in LA. Partners get listed, receive all incoming leads, and experience the platform at zero cost. Goal: build trust and prove the lead quality before asking for a dollar.
Target: 15 partners · $0 MRR
02
Phase 2 · Month 3 — Month 6
Soft Launch Pricing
Once real leads are flowing (minimum 20–30 client requests/month), approach existing free partners with a founder rate. Offer a permanent discount of 30–40% off standard pricing as a thank-you for being early. New partners onboard at full price. This creates urgency and rewards loyalty.
Target: 20 partners · $4K–$8K MRR
03
Phase 3 · Month 6 — Month 12
Full Monetization
Standard pricing active for all new partners. Expand beyond LA — Orange County, Las Vegas, Miami, New York. Introduce the 5% success fee for Pro and Elite tiers. Begin co-branded marketing with top partners (featured in NexAssist campaigns and social media). Launch partner dashboard with analytics.
Target: 50 partners · $20K–$40K MRR
04
Phase 4 · Year 2+
Scale & Enterprise
Introduce enterprise/custom plans for national providers (private jet operators, luxury hotel chains, concierge travel agencies). Add white-label options. Build API for direct integration into partner booking systems. Explore international expansion — Dubai, London, Marbella.
Target: 200+ partners · $100K+ MRR
